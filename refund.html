Refund Policy

Last updated: April 27, 2026

Echelon engagements are billed monthly in advance via Paddle. Because our services involve dedicated time and resources allocated at the start of each billing cycle, our refund policy reflects the nature of professional service delivery.

1. General Policy

All monthly retainer fees are non-refundable once a billing period has begun. By subscribing to any Echelon service tier, you acknowledge that work allocation and resource planning begins immediately upon payment.

2. Eligibility for Refunds

Refunds may be issued in the following limited circumstances:

  • Duplicate charge — If you were billed more than once for the same period due to a billing error, the duplicate charge will be refunded in full within 5 business days.
  • Charge before agreed start date — If your subscription was charged before the agreed engagement start date, a pro-rated refund for the unused days will be issued.
  • Service not delivered — If Echelon is unable to begin or materially deliver services for a given period through no fault of the client, a pro-rated refund will be issued for the undelivered portion.

3. Cancellation

You may cancel your engagement at any time by providing 30 days written notice to Laurenocampo.biz@gmail.com. Cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing period. No partial refunds are issued for mid-cycle cancellations.

For the Build tier (3-month minimum), early termination before the minimum term is complete does not entitle the client to a refund of fees already paid for the remaining contracted period.

4. Overage Charges

Overage fees ($800 per additional cycle) are billed in arrears at the end of the month in which the overage occurred. If you dispute an overage charge, contact us within 7 days of the invoice date. Approved disputes will be credited to the next billing cycle rather than refunded directly, unless the engagement has ended.

5. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, email Laurenocampo.biz@gmail.com with the subject line "Refund Request" and include:

  • Your full name and company name
  • The billing date and amount in question
  • A brief description of the reason for the request

We will review and respond within 5 business days. Approved refunds are processed via Paddle and may take 5–10 business days to appear depending on your bank or card issuer.

6. Disputes via Paddle

Paddle acts as the Merchant of Record for all Echelon transactions. If you initiate a chargeback or dispute directly with your bank before contacting us, we will cooperate fully with Paddle's dispute resolution process. We encourage clients to reach out to us first — most issues can be resolved quickly without a formal dispute.

7. Contact

Questions about billing or refunds? Email Laurenocampo.biz@gmail.com and we'll sort it out.
